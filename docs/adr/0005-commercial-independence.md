# ADR 0005: Commercial Independence

**Status:** Accepted  
**Date:** 12 July 2026

## Context

The same AI vendors and providers covered by the platform may become advertisers, sponsors, or affiliate partners.

## Decision

Commercial relationships must remain structurally separate from evidence ratings, rankings, and editorial conclusions.

ADR 0011 defines the enforceable commercial, disclosure, placement, audit and affiliate controls that implement this decision.

## Rules

- Advertising inventory is managed independently from editorial scoring.
- Ranking algorithms cannot access affiliate commission values.
- Sponsored content is stored and rendered as a distinct content type.
- Every commercial relationship must be disclosed.
- Negative findings cannot be removed in exchange for payment.
- Corrections are evidence-based only.

## Consequences

- Some high-paying commercial arrangements may be rejected.
- Trust remains a core product asset.
- Data models and admin permissions must preserve separation.
