# Solo-Founder Continuity Plan

The project begins with a bus factor of one.

## Controls

- Keep architecture, operations, deployment, source policy, and recovery procedures in the repository.
- Use infrastructure-as-code and automated deployments where practical.
- Maintain encrypted backups, recovery codes, and documented service ownership.
- Store source definitions declaratively.
- Avoid undocumented production changes.
- Keep recurring database exports and service inventories.
- Maintain runbooks for source failures, corrections, deployment, and disaster recovery.

## First future roles to add

1. Editorial/research reviewer
2. Backend/data engineer
3. Security/infrastructure reviewer
4. Commercial partnerships support

## Hiring triggers

Seek recurring help when:

- Paid users depend on continuity
- Active sources exceed 250
- High-risk review exceeds approximately 20 items per week
