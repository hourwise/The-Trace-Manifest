# Editorial Independence Policy

## Principle

The Trace Manifest exists to improve understanding of AI claims, evidence, and change. Commercial relationships must never alter editorial conclusions.

## Prohibited influence

Advertisers, sponsors, affiliates, partners, and paying vendors may not:

- Purchase higher evidence ratings
- Purchase benchmark placement
- Change rankings
- Suppress negative findings
- Remove corrections
- Rewrite conclusions
- Influence confidence labels
- Require favourable coverage
- Purchase exclusion of competitors

## Permitted commercial activity

Commercial partners may purchase:

- Clearly labelled advertising
- Clearly labelled sponsorship
- Newsletter placement
- Event sponsorship
- Directory visibility
- Recruitment listings
- Access to paid products or data

## Disclosure

Commercial relationships must be disclosed near affected content.

Where a ranked recommendation contains affiliate links, the page should state:

- Which providers have affiliate relationships
- Which do not
- That affiliate status does not affect scoring
- The date the comparison was last reviewed

## Sponsored content

Sponsored material must:

- Be clearly labelled
- Be visually distinct
- Identify the sponsor
- Avoid imitating independent editorial analysis
- Link to this policy

## Corrections

Commercial partners have the same right to submit corrections as any other source. Correction requests must be assessed against evidence, not commercial status.
