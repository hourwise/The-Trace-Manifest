# Monetisation Strategy

## Objective

Cover infrastructure, data, email, and AI-processing costs early, then fund better research and product features without compromising trust.

## Early stage

- Supporter membership
- Voluntary contributions
- One direct sponsor
- Newsletter sponsor
- Carefully disclosed affiliate links
- House ads for related projects
- Small paid reports
- Limited professional ask-box plan

## Growth stage

- Contextual developer advertising
- Paid watchlists
- Price and licence alerts
- Premium briefings
- Professional reports
- Job listings
- Data exports
- Team accounts

## Mature stage

- Commercial API
- White-label feeds
- Enterprise monitoring
- Private dashboards
- Procurement research
- Custom benchmark programmes
- Security and governance reports
- Research partnerships

## Suggested plans

### Free

- Public feed
- Daily briefing
- Basic model and benchmark pages
- Limited ask-box use
- Basic alerts

### Supporter

Indicative price: £3–£5/month

- Ad-free browsing
- More questions
- Saved history
- Watchlists
- Weekly supporter briefing
- Markdown export

### Professional

Indicative price: £10–£20/month

- Deeper research
- Longer date ranges
- Custom comparisons
- Advanced alerts
- CSV and PDF export
- Personalised recommendations

### Team

Indicative price: £40–£100/month

- Shared workspaces
- Team watchlists
- Shared research
- Administration
- Higher usage limits
- Webhooks
- Team exports

## Commercial rules

- Sponsors buy visibility, not conclusions.
- Affiliate links must be disclosed.
- Rankings cannot depend on commission.
- No hidden native advertising.
- No pay-to-remove-negative-findings.
- No paid benchmark manipulation.

## ADR 0011 implementation boundary

Commercial activity remains a later, separately enabled workstream. Until its required controls are implemented, TRACE must not enable behavioural or cross-site tracking, paid editorial inclusion, paid rankings, automatic affiliate-link rewriting, or advertising inside Ask TRACE, evidence, citation, correction, or confidence components.

App Radar, Open Source Radar, Spotlight and TRACE Guides may not sell normal editorial inclusion. Any permitted affiliate material must deliver original value, name the relationship before the link, retain non-affiliate alternatives, and never alter technical instructions, evidence status, or ranking.

See [ADR 0011](../adr/0011-advertising-sponsorship-affiliate-marketing-and-commercial-implementation.md) and the [editorial independence policy](../trust/editorial-independence.md).
