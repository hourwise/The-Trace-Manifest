# Monetisation Strategy

## Objective

Cover infrastructure, data, email, and AI-processing costs early, then fund better research and product features without compromising trust.

## Early stage

- Supporter membership
- Voluntary contributions
- One direct sponsor
- Newsletter sponsor
- Carefully disclosed affiliate links
- House ads for related projects
- Small paid reports
- Limited professional ask-box plan

## Growth stage

- Contextual developer advertising
- Paid watchlists
- Price and licence alerts
- Premium briefings
- Professional reports
- Job listings
- Data exports
- Team accounts

## Mature stage

- Commercial API
- White-label feeds
- Enterprise monitoring
- Private dashboards
- Procurement research
- Custom benchmark programmes
- Security and governance reports
- Research partnerships

## Suggested plans

### Free

- Public feed
- Daily briefing
- Basic model and benchmark pages
- Limited ask-box use
- Basic alerts

### Supporter

Indicative price: £3–£5/month

- Ad-free browsing
- More questions
- Saved history
- Watchlists
- Weekly supporter briefing
- Markdown export

### Professional

Indicative price: £10–£20/month

- Deeper research
- Longer date ranges
- Custom comparisons
- Advanced alerts
- CSV and PDF export
- Personalised recommendations

### Team

Indicative price: £40–£100/month

- Shared workspaces
- Team watchlists
- Shared research
- Administration
- Higher usage limits
- Webhooks
- Team exports

## Commercial rules

- Sponsors buy visibility, not conclusions.
- Affiliate links must be disclosed.
- Rankings cannot depend on commission.
- No hidden native advertising.
- No pay-to-remove-negative-findings.
- No paid benchmark manipulation.
